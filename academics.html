Major Sample Plan

A comprehensive 8-semester journey designed to master everything from financial accounting to advanced consumer analytics modeling.

Year 01

Fall Semester 14 Credits

  • ECON 1022 Principles of Economics: Macro
  • MATH 1160 Finite Mathematics & Calculus
  • PSY 1003 General Psychology
  • LSBE 1101 Business & Society

Spring Semester 15 Credits

  • MATH 2210 Applied Calculus
  • COMM 1112 Public Speaking
  • WRIT 1120 College Writing
  • UST 1000 Learning in Community

Year 02

Fall Semester 15 Credits

  • ACCT 2001 Principles of Financial Accounting
  • ECON 1023 Principles of Economics: Micro
  • BLAW 2001 The Legal Environment
  • LE Global Perspectives

Spring Semester 16 Credits

  • ACCT 2002 Principles of Managerial Accounting
  • ECON 2030 Applied Stats for Business
  • MIS 2201 IT in Business
  • LE Cultural Diversity in the US

Year 03

Fall Semester 15 Credits

  • CIA 3760 Intro to Consumer Insights
  • BA 4410 Data Visualization
  • MKTG 3701 Principles of Marketing
  • BCOM 3141 Business Communications

Spring Semester 16 Credits

  • CIA 4761 Analytic Techniques
  • FIN 3601 Corporate Finance
  • MIS 3220 Database Management
  • MGTS 3301 Production & Ops Management
  • LSBE 3000 Career Development
