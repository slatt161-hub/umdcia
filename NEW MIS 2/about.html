Mission & Vision

"Data is the language. Insight is the story. Action is the goal."

insights

Our Mission

To develop students to transform consumer data related to buying behaviors into insights and actionable recommendations for organizations. We empower our students to confidently present their insights as solutions to complex business problems.

DATA SCIENCE MARKETING ETHICS

Our Vision

To help organizations make better strategic decisions based on data rather than opinion, while deeply studying why and how consumers interact with the marketplace, products, and services.

Our Cohort Approach

As decision-making becomes more data-driven, the Consumer Insights & Analytics major sets a new standard with a highly selective application process and dedicated cohort approach. Each cohort completes specialized courses together, building strong professional relationships.

We partner directly with leading corporations on active, real-world industry projects. Our sponsors have included powerhouses like Hormel, Target, maurices, and Schwan’s Food Company. Students have 24/7 access to our state-of-the-art analytics lab, utilizing tools like Excel, PowerBI, Tableau, SQL, SAS, R, and Python.

From acceptance to graduation, Dedicated Program Director **Steve Sharkey** acts as a coach and mentor, ensuring students are perfectly positioned for internships that often turn into full-time roles before their senior year even concludes.
